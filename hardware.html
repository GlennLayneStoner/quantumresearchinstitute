Recommended Hardware

Your phone can spark ideas. A laptop helps you finish them. Start where you are and grow from there.

Start Where You Are

Capture ideas on your phone, outline with the QRI Guide, save sources to Zotero, and when you are ready for deeper work move to a laptop or desktop. The goal is progress, not perfection.

Tip: write on mobile, organize references with Zotero, then model or simulate on a laptop.

Essential Software

  • Writing: LibreOffice Writer, Google Docs, or Word
  • Browser: Chrome, Brave, or Firefox
  • References: Zotero desktop + browser connector
  • Quantum simulation: QuTiP with Python 3.10 via Anaconda or Miniconda
  • Code & notebooks: VS Code, Jupyter, or Spyder
  • Optional: GitHub Desktop, Overleaf
